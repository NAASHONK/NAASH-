spaghetti Bogoognese

Ingredients:

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 100g ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 400g canned tomatoes
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil leaves

Instructions:

    l
  1. Cook the spaghetti according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large pan, sauté the onion and garlic until soft.
  3. Add the ground beef and cook until browned.
  4. Stir in the canned tomatoes and let it simmer for 15 minutes.
  5. Season with salt and pepper, then mix in the cooked spaghetti.
  6. Garnish with fresh basil leaves before serving.

Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 cup chocolate chips

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Cream together the butter and sugar in a bowl.
  3. In a separate bowl, combine the flour, baking soda, and salt.
  4. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the butter mixture, mixing until smooth.
  5. Fold in the chocolate chips.
  6. Drop spoonfuls of dough onto a baking sheet and bake for 10-12 minutes.
  7. Allow cookies to cool before serving.
